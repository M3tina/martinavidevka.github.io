Big Hit Entertainment

To become genuinely acquainted with BTS as a K-pop group, their music and what they stand for in today's ever-changing music industry you must grasp the major role their label company has had in their success. BigHit Entertainment was established in 2005 by Bang Si-hyuk. The first couple of years the company didn't report a huge success, but that was starting to change as the company first signed RM or Kim Namjoon as a trainee. They would soon assemble the lucky seven boys and produce the biggest music act the world has ever seen. BigHit employees took a big gamble with BTS, but they believed that much in these seven boys that they took loan after loan, worked overtime to help the passionate boys thrive in the South Korean music industry.

This is the building BTS was formed.

And their success managed to get their small underdog company to this new building.
