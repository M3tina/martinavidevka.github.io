Meet the members

The major reason for BTS's success is without a doubt the boys that represent it. These seven men are the most exceptional, humble, talented and over all amazing humans being who love their fans greatly.

  • RM

    Birthdy: September 12th, 1994
    Position: Leader, Rapper

    RM is the reason BTS exist today. With hard work, unwavering passion and excellent leadership, RM and the rest of his members went on to become one of the most sought after artists of their generation, known not just in South Korea but all over the world.

  • Jin

    Birthdy: December 4th, 1992
    Position: Sub Vocalist, Visual

    Jin was just walking down a street when he was spotted by a casting agent and was invited to audition for BigHit Ent. With a head-turner presence, sweet vocals, and acting education, Jin became a BTS member. He expresses his enormous love and appreciation for his fans releasing songs such as "Moon" and "Yours"

  • Suga

    Birthdy: March 9th, 1993
    Position: Lead Rapper

    He made his humble beginnings as an underground rapper with the stage name Gloss. Eventually, aside from rapping, SUGA also began to produce music at the age of 13. In 2016 he released his first solo mixtape, "Agust D," under the alias Agust D. Now he is one of the most sought after producers in the korean music industry.

  • J-Hope

    Birthdy: February 18th, 1994
    Position: Main Dancer, Lead Vocalist

    J-hope first passion was dancing. His talent landed him a spot in BTS. J-Hope released his first solo mixtape, "Hope World" in 2018, and became the highest-charting Korean soloist at the time on Billboard 200. His new mixtape is will be released on July 15th. J-Hope is announced to headline "Lollapalooza" on July 31st. His performance will make history, cementing him as the first South Korean artist to headline a main stage at a major U.S. music festival.

  • Jimin
    Birthday: October 13th, 1995
    Position: Main Dancer, Lead Vocalist

    Jimin was a top student in the modern dance department at Busan High School when one of his teachers suggested him to audition for an entertainment company. In december of 2018 Jimin released a single that broke the record for the biggest 24-hour debut in history.

  • V

    Birthday: December 30th, 1995
    Position: Sub Vocalist, Visual

    When he was just fifteen years old, V accompanied his friend to audition at BigHit Ent. He was only there to support his friend, but an executive noticed him and encouraged him to audition. He ended up passing the audition and became an official trainee. Besides performing, V also took on acting projects. He debuted as an actor in late 2016.

  • Jungkook

    Birthday: September 1st, 1997
    Position: Main Vocalist, Lead Dancer

    After watching RM perform, Jungkook chose to be a part of BigHit Ent. He is the well-rounded maknae, extremely talented in singing, dancing and rapping. He collaborated with prominent artists from the west such as Charlie Puth for their new single "Left and Right".
