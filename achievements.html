BTS Achievements

After spending few years at the top, BTS has become their biggest competition. They are daily breaking and setting new record both in the home entertainment scene and international. Here are some of their most notable achievements.

Giving a speech at the White House

On June 1st BTS were invited to the White House to talk to the president and address a very important contemporary issue regarding anti-Asian hate.

GRAMMYs performance and nomination

For three years in a row the fans of BTS were rewarded with a breathtaking performance by the boys on the GRAMMY stage. The ARMYs (the name for BTS's fans) were thrilled to discover the news of BTSs well deserved nomination in 2020 and 2021 for a GRAMMY alongside extremely prominent artists from the western music industry. Unfortunately, these seven boys from South Korea were taken advantage of and not treated with the same respect as western artist are.

Top social Artist

The Top Social Artist award at the AMAs has become BTSs own category. They haven't lost the title of top social artist since their first win in 2017.

Youtube records

24 hours after its release, "Butter" also broke the record for the most viewed YouTube music video in 24 hours with 108,200,000 views.
