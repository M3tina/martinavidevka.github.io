MEET BTS

Enless you have been living under a rock, you must have heard about the global phenomenon taking the world by storm currently, BTS.

BTS an acronym of Bangtan Sonyeondan or Beyond the Stage is the biggest group in the world. Ever since their debut, K-pop boy band BTS — comprised of Jungkook, Jimin, V, Suga, Jin, J-Hope and RM — has stolen the hearts of millions of people around the world. We mean, with their catchy songs, angelic voices, to-die-for looks and swoon-worthy dance moves, it’s pretty hard not to become obsessed with them.
The members of BTS are gaining recognition from their authentic, self-produced music and the main top-notch performances, that leaves people speechless, to the unique way they interact with their fans the band is well on their way to becoming our global icon since they debuted in June 2013.

ABOUT BTS

BTS is a K-pop group under the entertainment company BigHit Ent. Considering the fact that they started under the rules of K-pop each member has gone through audition and trainee period. BigHit Ent at the start of BTS's career was an underdog compared to the extremely powerful and much more successful companies at that time in South Korea. But the company believed in BTS and their talent and passion and that is why today that same company is the largest record label in the world.
Their start wasn't intrinsically successful, but the boys had an exceptional love for music and performing (and they still do of course) that gave them hope and comfort to keep going and never stop. Their hardships and suffering now proved not to be in wain as they are enjoying the greatest success a musician and a performer ever had. BTS is breaking new records every day. Their biggest competition is themselves, witch speaks volumes for their triumphs and accomplishments.
